FRIED CHICKEN RECIPE

This is a tasty dish with chicken that's crunchy on the outside and soft inside. The chicken is covered in a crispy coat and fried until it's golden. It's really good when it's hot and goes great with things like salad or fries. People love it because it's yummy and comforting. Perfect for sharing with friends or having a fun meal at home.

List of ingredients

  • 500g of chicken thighs
  • 3 egg
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 Tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 2 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • Vegetable Oil

How to make fried chicken

  • Beat the eggs in a big bowl
  • Make seasoned salt, combine paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl
  • Fill a third bowl with flour
  • Season each piece of chicken with seasoned salt
  • Dip each piece in the egg and drag through flour until coated well
  • Fill a large pot or deep fryer half full with oil and heat to 350°F/110°C
  • Place the chicken parts in and fry for 15 minutes
